About Them

MMMM are five data life forms from outer space, composed of pixels and stardust. They can appear or disappear instantly through communication devices. The ears on their heads are magnetic antennas that can be used as communication tools.

Their hobby is to rummage through computer recycle bins, and perhaps they can help you retrieve accidentally deleted files. One day, while playing in the recycle bin, MMMM seemed to have accidentally touched some disordered code and transformed into the appearance of a 0.8-year-old baby...
